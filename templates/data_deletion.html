Option 2: Request via Email

If you can't access the app, you can request account deletion via email:

  1. Send an email to: support@connect.io

What Gets Deleted?

  • Your user profile information
  • Chat history and messages
  • Uploaded photos and files
  • Account preferences and settings
  • Friends list and connections

Important Information

Deletion is permanent: Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered.

Processing time: Complete data removal from our servers may take up to 30 days.

Legal retention: We may retain certain data as required by law (e.g., for fraud prevention).
